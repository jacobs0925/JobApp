A Little About Me:
Hello! I'm a college graduate from University of California Irvine with a Bachelor's in Computer Science of Engineering. I'm a passionate and self-driven developer and am looking for an opportunity to work in a professional setting and hone my skills with the help of a team. I have experience with Python, Java, Javascript, and other website development tools.

I started my programming journey when I was 13 and would write plugins for my favorite game at the time, Minecraft. Not very serious at first, I made fun add-ons for myself and friends and eventually wanted to reach more people with my work. I began reaching out to server owners and soon was developing for pay. Although I felt at the time it wasn't "real" programming, it was my first introduction to Object Oriented Programming and to development with respect to a client's needs.

I continued with plugin development for a couple more years until I reached college where it took a back seat. I was introduced to Python, APIs, and more advanced computer science principles. With my knowledge of python I began making scripts, again for my self and others. Some of my favorites were a bot for Reddit.com, a text-message-controlled stock trading script, and an mp3 downloader for a roommate who was into music production.

During my summers I began working at D&D Security where I would unpackage, tag, and flash software onto Chromebooks for a local school district. Although it was nice to work with computers I wanted to write code professionally so after a few years and talking to my manager, I became D&D's Salesforce developer. I was introduced to relational databases as well as some basic HTML, JS, and CSS. With this experience I began making my own simple websites and today I am a freelance web developer.
